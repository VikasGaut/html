What is so great about 'Breaking Bad' ?

  1. It’s Visually Stunning
    • The cinematography is just perfect. The colors and environment look awesome.
      The series is filmed in New Mexico and the set-up is way above the normal TV standards.
  2. Perfect Screenplay
    • You won't find a spot which is just pushed for unnecessary filling. The dialogues are
      tight and the acting is perfect. You won't find a single scene where the acting is not okay!
      The scenes are perfectly filmed and are very realistic.
  3. Love of Science!
    • Science is in the soul of this show. This show will teach you how to make bombs, metal
      cutters and the crystal meth by using easily available chemicals (just kidding!). But, you'll
      appreciate the scientific accuracy of this show.
  4. The Adrenaline moments
      5. The series is filled with such situations which will make you bite your nails. The tension,
      the thrill and the final blow will really blow your mind!
  5. The Ending
    • This show has the best ending ever. Believe me, the last episode of the series is just the
      perfect. I won't reveal anything but you will feel satisfied at the end.In conclusion, I
      would say that anyone who enjoys great story, good screenplay, perfect acting, technical and
      visual brilliance should watch this series.
